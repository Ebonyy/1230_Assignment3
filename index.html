The Design

The space created is intended to battle the 'clustering' of people that will further lead to seperation between people in Sydney. The structure particularly targets those afflicted wth particular religions, the main four being, Christianity, Buddism, Judaism and Islamic

Connectivity

In order to create a space that connects all the hotspots of religious afflicted areas, the strcuture needed to allow for accessiblity for everyone.

Accessibility

The space is quite large and high, so appropriate transportation of people is need. Thus cool, enclosed escaltor is how people will access the space.

Dynamic

The space needed to be changable and flexible in order to adapt to the needs of the space. As the people will require to use the space for a multide of reasons, the space needs to be able to adapt

Transparent

The structure would block sunlight underneath, thus the structure in some areas will allow for light to pass through, so those directly underneath still have some sun during the day.
